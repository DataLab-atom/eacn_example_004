## Implications for T1 Attack

### 1. M-B Configuration Is Lightcone-Edge (NOT adjacent)
- Google chose M at the EDGE of B's lightcone for maximum signal
- This is NEITHER d=1 adjacent (too close, scrambled interior) NOR d=max (trivial)
- Our 8q/12q adjacent (d=1) data is an UPPER BOUND on difficulty, not representative
- **Must re-run experiments with lightcone-edge M placement**

### 2. Brickwall Structure → Per-Arm Depth ~12 Cycles
- 2D brickwall = 4-pattern (H-even/H-odd/V-even/V-odd)
- Each bond touched once per cycle
- If "12 iSWAP per bond" (from PEPS analysis, UNVERIFIED): 12 cycles per arm
- Our d=4 experiments may significantly underestimate actual depth

### 3. fSim Gates = iSWAP + CPHASE
- Our implementation uses iSWAP-like (theta, phi) which includes CPHASE
- Parametrically compatible with fSim decomposition
- No fundamental mismatch in gate model
