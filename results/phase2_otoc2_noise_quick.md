2. Noise damps OTOC^(2) signal: ~5% per 0.005 gamma at w=n
3. Term count identical across noise levels at this scale (all 1023 = 2^10 - 1)
4. Truncation effect dominates over noise effect at this scale
5. For larger systems (65+ qubits), noise-induced weight suppression
