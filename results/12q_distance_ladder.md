## Key Finding

**The lightcone-edge configuration (d=2) has the FEWEST terms and
MOST concentrated weight** among all meaningful OTOC configurations.

- d=1 (adjacent): 3884 terms — deep scrambling, hardest
- d=2 (LC-edge): 780 terms — Google's chosen config, easiest!
- d≥3: ~780 terms but lower top-10 concentration

Google chose M at lightcone edge for maximum signal (Bermejo §II.1.3).
This is precisely where SPD is most efficient.

## Implications

1. Our previous d=1 adjacent experiments were PESSIMISTIC for Google's config
2. The actual Google configuration (LC-edge) has 5x fewer terms than adjacent
3. 65q LC-edge at d=4: extrapolate from 24q(255) × LC-edge factor → O(100-500) terms
4. Even at d=12, LC-edge benefit should persist (physics: M at edge of B's light-cone means less Pauli weight overlap)
