## Revised Understanding

The real question isn't "M-B distance" but "has B(t) scrambled enough to reach M?"
- If depth < distance(M,B): OTOC ≈ 1 (no scrambling yet), easy for SPD
- If depth ≥ distance(M,B): OTOC < 1, harder for SPD, more terms needed

For Willow: the INTERESTING OTOC measurements are those where scrambling HAS occurred.
These are the hard cases — and they require higher truncation weight.
