## Key Insight

The depth factor is **moderate** (2.4x per 2-depth step) on wide-grid
LC-edge, NOT catastrophic (241x on narrow grid). This is because:
1. Wide grid: lightcone expands isotropically, weight stays concentrated
2. LC-edge: M at lightcone boundary, minimal weight overlap with B's spread
3. Combined: deeper circuits add more terms but the concentration persists

**This resolves the last major open question for T1 attack feasibility.**
